HISTORY

At 5 years old, started my first scrap book and began learning to paint. Gained a reputation in the family for the person who made cards for every celebration. Later that same year, my painting submission won second place in the town’s art fair.

Participated in my first art show, showcasing visual and digital artworks, including a 18.5” x 21” photorealistic graphite drawing and oil painting, a landscape layered painting, a dress sewn in two days, and a projected digital illustration.

Graduated Saint André Bessette High School the only Yearbook Designer and Illustrator, as well as a leader of the book club, robotics team member, student chapel member, and announcements speaker. Started and ran the school’s first art club, being the only student leader. Received awards in Visual Arts 11 and 12, completing both courses in the same year, and Marketing.

Presently studying at Waterloo University’s Global Business and Digital Arts’s remote campus building in Stratford, Ontario. Building up my knowledge of relevant industry tech and software with projects and learning guided by industry-experienced instructors.
