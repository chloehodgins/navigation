HISTORY

At 5 years old, started my first scrap book and began learning to paint. Gained a reputation in the family for the person who made cards for every celebration. Later that same year, my painting submission won second place in the town’s art fair.


Participated in my first art show, showcasing visual and digital artworks, including a 18.5” x 21” photorealistic graphite drawing and oil painting, a landscape layered painting, a dress sewn in two days, and a projected digital illustration.


Graduated Saint André Bessette High School the only Yearbook Designer and Illustrator, as well as a leader of the book club, robotics team member, student chapel member, and announcements speaker. Started and ran the school’s first art club, being the only student leader. Received awards in Visual Arts 11 and 12, completing both courses in the same year, and Marketing.

present day

What's on my

MIND

Hobbies
Fashion
What began as a love for playing dress-up developed into a passion for fashion design. I started with fashion sketching, experimenting with pairing clothing and jewelry around my home, and progressed to exploring fashion photography. Eventually, I began creating my own print designs and clothing.
Arts
Since my childhood of pencil sketching, scrapbooking, jewelry making, and painting I have received a formal education in art and now design. My interests in art now additionally include cinematography, animation, printmaking, 3D printing, comic illustration, and graphic design.
Sports
When it comes to sports I have several to choose from, including skiing, rollerblading, ice skating, cycling, and golfing. I was previously a long-distance marathon runner, alongside regularly playing soccer and basketball. These days, I regularly go to the gym, swim, and run.
Music
A love for singing and acting led to my interest in theatre and landing my first lead role in a production of the Wizard of Oz, as Dorothy. Continued acting alongside learning to play the piano and acoustic guitar.
Interests
Reading
I enjoy a variety of book genres, including historical fiction, mystery, graphic novels, and comics. Some of my favorite books and series are The Thief Lord, Harry Potter, Macbeth, Assassin’s Curse, and Secrets. My favorite authors include Neil Gaiman, David Walliams, and Dr. Seuss.
Language Learning
I am fully fluent in English, French, and Tagalog, with additional experience in Spanish and Korean.
Design
I admire the work of fashion designers Robert Wun and Tamara Ralph, along with my favorite print designs by Areesha Khalid. When it comes to historical art, Vincent Van Gogh, Claude Monet, and Frida Kahlo are among my inspirations. For timeless animated filmmaking, I appreciate the artistry of Studio Ghibli, Gobelins, and classic Disney productions.
